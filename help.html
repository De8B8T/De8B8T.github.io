Current Commands

-info

Displays information about the bot, including the number of successful deliveries.

-cmds

Provides a small list of available commands.

-kit [name]

Delivers a fast kit based on the specified name. Aliases available.

e.g. -kit pvp

-kithelp [pvp/build]

Displays available kits based on the category:

PVP Kits:

  • PVP: Aliases: pvp, spvp
  • CPVP: Aliases: crystalpvp, cpvp
  • Health: Aliases: health, hpvp, totems, totem, gaps
  • Refill: Aliases: refill, armorrefill
  • Ammo Refill: Aliases: amrefill, ammorefill
  • Experience: Aliases: xp, mend, experience
  • Elytra: Aliases: elytra, ely, flight

Build Kits:

  • Stone: Aliases: build, stone, cstone
  • Wood: Aliases: wood, cwood, signs
  • Concrete: Aliases: cconcrete, concrete, misc
  • Duplication: Aliases: duplication, zombie, dupe
  • Redstone: Aliases: redstone, red, electric
  • Nether Mix: Aliases: nethermix, cnether, nether

-fact

Private messages you a random fact from the API.

-quote

Private messages you a random quote from the API.

-flip

Private messages you a random side of a coin (heads/tails).

-roll [num1] [num2]

Private messages you a random number from [num1] to [num2] (like rolling a dice).

e.g. -roll 1 6

-is [username]

Checks if a player is online or offline based on their username.

e.g. -is PlayerOne
